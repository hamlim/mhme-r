#YOLOSWAG: Working

Link

Messing Around with Media

Probably one of my first projects that I finished, #YOLOSWAG was a simple single webpage application designed to be as simple as possible. Everyonce and a while when I found something interesting that I wanted to share, I would make a hashtag for it, and add it to the site and also play music in the background. For me this project taught me about using the simple HTML tag. Really this project was more about the content then the actual web page.

Trending News

Currentwire was a small spring break hacking project during Sophomore year that turned out taking almost a full year to design it and extend it to what I wanted. I have far more goals for the project that I decided to put on the backburner because of classes. This project mostly taught me about asynchronous javascript and using the twitter API. The central reason I didn't come back to working on this project ful time was because I don't see it as a significant source of value.

Afternoon Coffee: Working

Link

Feedback on Ideas

Probably one of my claims to fame in a few years from now will be the fact that I had the idea to use Slack as a recreation of public IRC channels right before everyone else was jumping on the bandwagon. Sure I can't prove it, but I think I had the idea formed in some sense a few days before communities like #startup started up. Afternoon Coffee is a Slack team open to anyone to get feedback on their product and startup ideas. You can think of it as a way to communicate with others to provide valuable feedback on ideas you might have.

Link

The Future of Forge Labs and Myself

The idea of Listen List came about from a discussion with my dad and brother. I have written about it a few times in my personal blog, and will be writing a lot more about the project in the future. The core behind Listen List is what will be Forge Labs' future business structure. If you want to know more about that you will have to check out a future blog post. Listen List is a simple social network, sort of like a Producthunt for podcasts. I am still building it and I plan on continuing to build it through 2015 and farther.
