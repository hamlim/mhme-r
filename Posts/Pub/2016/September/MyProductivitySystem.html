My Productivity System

A quick look at some thoughts on my productivity system

Published: 2016-09-05

In my eyes, every productivity system has to handle three core things for the individual. These three things can be handled in different applications or different forms but I believe when they share the same rough format or traits it makes it a lot easier to manage and use. The three core things that a productivity system should handle are:

  1. Notes
  2. Tasks
  3. Events

I used to be all about life hacking and working on my productivity systems. I still do switch to new task managers about every week because I still havn't found something that works the way I want it to.
