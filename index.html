Currentwire

Tackling the News

Currentwire was one of my culminating experiences with the Twitter API in full scale and although I did not get the growth of the site as I expected or wanted, I did learn about many limitations in the API and things about Javascript that I did not know of before. I still sometimes go to Curratd to look up some trending news tweets, and was highly interested in opportunities of developing a pay to use version.

Although I don't think it is officailly done, I am confident that what I have developed so far makes Currentwire a successful product.

When is Better

Experiments in Team Time Management

When Is Better is a term project, turn full time development project with a group of fantastic individuals from a course at RPI. We wanted to develop a web app that would handle the hard work with setting up team meetings. We always thought that scheduling team meetings are one sided so we decided to change that. As I said at the beginning of the paragraph, many of this stuff will be changing soon, so expect developments on twitter or in my blog!
