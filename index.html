My Projects

This site is dedicated to showing off some of the work I have done on the web and also for representing my Resume and some of the content in my Blog. Everything here is made by me, yet through the use of a lot of technologies developed by others. Feel free to look through my work and my thoughts!

#YOLOSWAG

Experiments in Media

#YOLOSWAG became a way for me to express the things I enjoyed experiencing around the internet in the previous days or so. It offered me a way to express some interesting link, in the form of a hashtag, to all my friends.

Currentwire

Tracking the News

Currentwire was one of my culminating experiences with the Twitter API in full scale and although I did not get the growth of the site as I expected or wanted, I did learn about many limitations in the API and things about Javascript that I did not know of before. I still sometimes go to Curratd to look up some trending news tweets, and was highly interested in opportunities of developing a pay to use version.

Afternoon Coffee

Creating Online Communities

Afternoon Coffee is a public Slack chat team designed for open discussion on product ideas, random startup topics, and general bootstrapping discussions. If you want to join in on the conversation feel free to email me to join the conversation!

Pace

Simple, Quick, and Visual Project Management

Pace is a project I originally developed with a team, and then redesigned and added on additional features to make it a useful project management solution. Visit the launch page here to get more information about Pace.

#Ultichats

More Online Communities

#ultichats is an idea that started with some conversations on twitter with other Ultimate associated twitter accounts. I realized that twitter isn't the best platform for discussing Ultimate, so I started a slack team for it. if you want to join, or get more information, check this out - here!

Listen List

A Podcast Sharing Community

There are several platforms used for sharing these days, however I haven't found too many platforms for recommending, shareing and discovering new great podcasts. So I decided that I would try to build one. Listen List is still in development, in fact as of January 2015, its not even functioning! If you want to find out more by sending me an email: listenlist@outlook.com.
